Shopify Integration is a powerful app designed to seamlessly connect your Shopify store with Odoo. It enables real-time synchronization of products, customers, orders, and inventory between both platforms. The app simplifies order fulfillment by automatically updating stock levels and pushing order statuses from Odoo to Shopify, allowing centralized management single Odoo instance. With detailed logs, scheduled import/export operations, and customizable mappings, this integration ensures efficient, accurate, and hassle-free eCommerce operations

What is the Shopify Integration app?
Shopify Integration is a dedicated Odoo app that seamlessly connects your Shopify store(s) with your Odoo backend. It automates the synchronization of products, orders, customers, and inventory, ensuring unified eCommerce and ERP operations.
How does the app assist with managing Shopify orders?
The app automatically imports orders from Shopify into Odoo, allowing you to manage deliveries, generate invoices, and process payments â€” all from within Odoo. This streamlines order fulfillment and reduces manual data entry.
Is my Shopify data secure?
Yes, data security is a priority. The integration uses secure APIs provided by Shopify and Odoo. No sensitive data is stored outside your system, and communication between platforms is encrypted.
Can I monitor sync logs and performance analytics?
Yes. The app provides detailed synchronization logs and performance metrics, allowing you to monitor sync history, identify issues quickly, and maintain operational transparency.
